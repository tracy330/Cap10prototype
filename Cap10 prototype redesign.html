Our Firm

Cap10 is a London-based private investment firm, founded in 2021 to bring large-cap sophistication, creativity and discipline to founder- and family-owned businesses across Western Europe — the companies seeking liquidity for ownership transitions, real opportunities for their management teams, and experienced partners for the next phase of growth.

What makes Cap10 different is that we are purposefully built to be a long-term partner rather than a short-term trader. We pair a patient, flexible approach to ownership — including continued participation for existing shareholders and meaningful equity for current and future management — with the operational experience, sector conviction and network that an ambitious business actually draws upon. We invest in four themes we know deeply: niche industrials, essential consumer, health & pharma, and infrastructure solutions. And we measure ESG as a value driver, not a marketing line.

Our firm

Large-cap sophistication, applied to the European mid-market.

Cap10 was founded in 2021 by experienced financial entrepreneurs who bring large-cap sophistication, creativity and innovation to the European mid-market buyout space. We deliver asymmetric returns to the upside, by transforming underdeveloped mid-cap business gems into industry champions.

We partner with talented entrepreneurs who share our vision for win-win transformations that build value for every stakeholder.

Our focus is specific. We typically invest in companies headquartered in Western Europe, with enterprise values between €100m and €500m, in four thematic sectors we know deeply: niche industrials, essential consumer, health & pharma, and infrastructure solutions. We look for businesses that challenge the status quo — often ones that underestimate the strength of their own position.

Our approach is operational. Moving at pace, we deliver value and transaction certainty that bring mutually beneficial outcomes to existing shareholders — whether that's a family succession, a carve-out from a corporate, or a founder's next chapter. We invest sweat alongside capital: commercial strategy, talent, M&A, operational improvement.

Our conviction on ESG is commercial. We view it as an opportunity for sustainable growth that unlocks the true potential of a business, establishing it as a purposeful leader in its industry. It is measured, reported, and compounded financially alongside the P&L.

The Sureserve Foundation was set-up to support individuals, families and communities and builds on Sureserve’s wider ESG targets.

The Sureserve Foundation was set-up in March 2019 with the purpose and objective of creating warmer communities through the alleviation of fuel poverty; by supporting individuals, families and communities to achieve fuel efficiency and in turn, lessen the financial burden of high energy bills.

Sureserve aim to work towards specific targets within the business, such as having 100% renewable energy offices by 2030, 100% zero emissions fleet by 2050 and having zero waste-to-landfill by 2030.

Deal announcement

Sureserve completes acquisition of Bonarius, expanding its European energy services platform.

10 November 2025

Sureserve, a leading UK-based provider of compliance and energy services for social housing and the public sector, has acquired the shares of Bonarius and its group companies.

This acquisition marks Sureserve’s eighth add-on investment supported by Cap10 and its first international step, since being acquired by Cap10 Partners through a public-to-private transaction in July 2023.

The acquisition of Bonarius is a strategic step in Sureserve’s international growth plans. Sureserve identified the Dutch market as a very attractive opportunity with many similarities to the UK. Bonarius offers Sureserve a unique entry point into the Dutch market to provide compliance and energy services to its core social housing customer segment, and a platform for further consolidation.

The current shareholders of Bonarius are enthusiastic about the future as the business enters into its next phase of growth under a new strategic ownership.

Sureserve intends to grow Bonarius by broadening its service offering, continuing to innovate sustainably, growing with its long-term and trusted existing clients, expanding regionally, and selectively pursuing acquisitions to build national presence. The Bonarius Academies in Zwanenburg and Zwolle will remain central to talent development and recruitment.

With Sureserve, Bonarius has found a supportive partner as it scales up its existing operations to ensure that the business can continue to deliver the highest quality of services and excel over the long term.

Fabrice Nottin, Founder & Managing Partner at Cap10 Partners added: “Cap10 has a strong focus on founder and family-led businesses, and we are very proud to support Sureserve in this transaction. We are grateful for the trust which the family and the Bonarius team placed in us to preserve and build upon their legacy.”
